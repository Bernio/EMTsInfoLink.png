Why You Need EMTLink

 

 

Who Should Use This Program

This program is helpful for:

1) a person living with a medical condition that can flare up at any time;

2) a person with a medical condition that can cause fainting spells or falling.

The program can give loved ones reassurance that informed care will be available in the event of an unforseen emergency.

To illustrate the usefulness of the program, a good friend who has an autoimmune disease that has weakened her kidneys, was taken to an emergency unit. The physician in charge decided that she should have a CT Scan enhanced with a contrast medium to provide a better image of her kidneys. The contrast medium, a metalic-based, iodinated dye provided the image, but caused such significant damage to her kidneys that she had to be placed on dialysis for the rest of her life. Had the physician realized that she had reduced kidney function by reviewing her past history, this could have been avoided!

A second example of a problem resulting from a lack of information--which would have been available using EMT Link--presented when an acquaintance entered an emergency situation and was immediately hydrated with a continuous infusion in a drip IV. In the case of a person with minimal kidney function, adding excess fluid to her compromised kidneys should not have been undertaken as it was bound to cause complications!
