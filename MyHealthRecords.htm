Information for Emergency Response Personnel

The information in My Health Records is assembled to help Emergency Response Personnel make immediate decisions on how to proceed with the care I need during an emergency situation. This information is confidential for use in my care and unique to me personally.

These files include the following categories of information:

1) my Personal Profile/Information

2) Emergency Contacts;

3) the names and contact information for my Doctors;

4) my Health Insurance Information;

5) my Medical History;

6) my Surgical History;

7) Inherited and Genetic Diseases;

8) What I am Allergic to;

9) Information on Implants and Cardiac Devices;

10) Vaccinations I have received

11) Medications/dosage I am taking.
