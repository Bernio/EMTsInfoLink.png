How Do I Set Up The Program

 

 

An Explanation of How to Set Up The Program

The forms listed below are linked to reference bases of information to help the person completing the forms copy and paste such things as symptoms, diseases, vaccines, implant devices, allergies, etc. The user is given a licence to use the online forms for life for the one time payment of $19.95 due at the time of enrollment in the program.

Once completed the twelve pages are encrypted, online-accessible and password protected. These forms are filled with a person's unique health information covering the following areas of interest to Paramedics during the delivery of Emergency Care:

1) a Personal Profile listing vital information;

2) the names and contact information of two Emergency Contacts (loved ones or close designated friends)

3) the names and contact information of primary/secondary physicians familiar with the patient and health issues;

4) the person's Health Insurance information;

5) the person's Medical History, including symptoms and diseases;

6) the person's Surgical History;

7) the person's genetic and/or inherited diseases;

8) the person's allergies, if any;

9) vaccinations received by the person;

10) a list of any implants including pacemakers, hips, etc.;

11) a list of medications being taken and dosages of each;

12) the name of the person's preferred hospital in the event admission becomes necessay
