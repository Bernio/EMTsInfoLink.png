Discussion Aids

These"discussion aids" help you develop key information about the medical disorder/disease you are experiencing to take to an office visit with your doctor. Development of an "Aid" facilitates a carefully thought-through selection of symptoms instead of "spontaneous" comments which may not fully describe the disorder/disease  being experienced. Learning about your symptoms and suggesting possible disorders/diseases helps you "Be Your Own Health Advocate" during a discussion with your doctor.

Start by selecting the anatomical area in which you are experiencing symptoms that you want to discuss with your doctor:
