What is EMT Link?

 

 

Why You May Want to Use This Program

EMT Link is a program that helps a person at risk of an unpredictable emergency set up a series of health records that can be accessed by a paramedic or emergency responder.

These records are designed to inform the emergency responder of the person's history of diseases, symptoms, surgeries, inherited or genetic diseases, allergies, implant information, vaccinations and medications being taken. This minimizes the risk of complications which may arise from the decisions on specific care administered while the patient is in a compromised situation.
