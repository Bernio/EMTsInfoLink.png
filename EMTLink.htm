EMT Link accesses a person's health records for the EMT during an emergency situation to help deliver informed care as needed.

The health records include the compromised person's:
1) Personal Profile;
2) Emergency Contact;
3) Doctors of record;
4) Health Insurance;
5) Medical and Surgical History;
6) Inherited/Genetic Diseases and Allergies;
7) Vaccinations and Implants information;
8) medications and dosages.
